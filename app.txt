Ok so this app is called Curately
This is for the homegrown brands who don't get much recogniion.
Brands can register themselves here.(ANy brand- clothing, accessories etc)


What is this app capable of doing?
1. There would be a list of all brands ranging for every category
2. A user can search for brands by tags like sustaninable,swimwear, formal etc and it can list all the brands for you.
3. A user can also search for a particular brand.
4. A user can also add a brand to their list.
5. There is an AI stylist which can suggest styles for you dpenedent on your taste, body type, etc
6. A user can also book an appointment with a stylist.
7. A user can also get a list of stylists in their area.
8. The ai stylist can suggest can suggest products for your preferences too
